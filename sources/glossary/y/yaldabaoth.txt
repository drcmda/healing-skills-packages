Yaldabaoth

Hebrew ילדאבהות (Literally "Children of the Void," from 
ילדה = yalda= child; 
בהו = bohu = void; 
אבהות = abbott = fatherhood, parentage, paternity). This term has positive and negative interpretation, for there are children of the Void above (the Ain Soph, Sunyata, or Emptiness) and children of the Void below (the Abyss). 

"The Light of Sophia always emerges from the sexual Chaos, and this Light shines in the Darkness. Sophia, as a Verb, is Yaldabaoth in complete action." - Samael Aun Weor, The Pistis Sophia Unveiled
