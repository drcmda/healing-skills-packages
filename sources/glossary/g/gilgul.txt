Gilgul

Hebrew גלגול "wheel, cycle." Plural: גלגולים‎ gilgulim. 
In Kabbalah, Gilgul 123 refers to how souls cycle in and out of bodies, what the Greeks called metempsychosis (Greek: μετεμψύχωσις) "transmigration of souls." Also called Gilgul neshamot or Gilgulei HaNeshamot (גלגול הנשמות‎) "cycle / wheel of souls."
