Zachariel

(Hebrew זכריאל, "remember God") The sixth of the seven angels. Related to Jupiter.  

"The Sixth Saviour of the emanations of the Sixth Voice of the Treasury of the Light, named Zachariel , will be in the region of the souls of those who have received the Sixth Mystery of the First Mystery." —Samael Aun Weor, The Gnostic Bible: The Pistis Sophia Unveiled 


“And the sixth angel (Zachariel) poured out his vial upon the river Euphrates; and the water thereof was dried up, that the way of the kings of the east might be prepared.” —Revelation 15:12
