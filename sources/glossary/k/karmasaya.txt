Karmasaya

(Sanskrit) Composed of karma-, "act, action, activity"; and -saya, "sexual desire, lust, passion" also "in the heart." 
The sum total of the impressions left by any sexual interrelationship (physical, emotional, and mental). In other words, impressions of past sexual encounters leave karmic traces within the subconsciousness (protoplasmic bodies / ego ) as a residual, potential memory; Karmasaya is the Karmic receptacle of the active effects of those impressions. 
Karmasaya is totally overcome through comprehension of its cause. Through in-depth Meditation on the great emotional crises originated by Karmasaya, comprehension arrives through the superlative Consciousness of the Being. Without crises and Meditation upon them, comprehension and subsequent elimination of Karmasaya are impossible. In such a case, Karmasaya remains within the mind as a cause of suffering.
