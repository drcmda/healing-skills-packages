Kuf

The nineteenth Letter of Hebrew alphabet, it symbolizes the Omnipresence of God and salvation of the fallen embryos of souls by means of the Omniscience of God . The Letter Kuf could be made of the combination of two letters: "Kaph" and " Vav " or "Reish" and "Zain." Each combination represents a different inclination of the soul. This letter is related with the sign of Jonah and the Kikaion (Pumpkin-Gourd in Hebrew ). Its Kabbalistic value is 100.
