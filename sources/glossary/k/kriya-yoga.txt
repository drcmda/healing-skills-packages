Kriya Yoga

1. In India and its yogas, Kriya is most known as the tradition related to
Paramahamsa Yogananda, which is a teaching consisting of Meditation and
pranayama (the "bellow" system). 
2. In the Tibetan tradition, there is
also a school of Yoga called Kriya, but it has little relationship to
the Indian versions. In this case Kriya Yoga is the entry level to the
higher forms of Tantra , and is called Action Tantra .
