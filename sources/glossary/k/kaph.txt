Kaph

The eleventh letter of the Hebrew alphabet, it symbolizes the crown chakra, in other words, it represents the Light-Power of the Ain Soph self-realizing its latent spiritual power in Chesed , the Monad, through the Three Supernals in the brain. The letter Kaph indicates the necessity of remembering the power of Elohim within us from moment to moment, so that the Three Supernals can fully manifest their power in our physical body and thus make their presence concrete within us. Its Kabbalistic value is 20.
