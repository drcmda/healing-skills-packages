Tlaloc

(the Aztec version of Chac) The Aztec and Mayan God of the rain. He was a main deity of the Aztecs and was worshipped at the Major Temple of Tenochtitlan. Related to the Kabbalistic sphere of Binah , Tlaloc is seen as the symbol of the Holy Spirit (rain comes from the heavens) pouring or unfolding into Yesod (the earth below) in order to bring abundance (spiritual and material). See: Binah
