Paramarthasatya

(Sanskrit) Para, “absolute, supreme.” Parama, “that which knows, or the
Consciousness .” Artha, “that which is known.” Satya, “existence,
Truth.” In synthesis, “The supreme knowledge of all that exists:
TRUTH.” 

1) A being of very high development; an inhabitant of the Absolute. 

2) The ultimate truth, as opposed to conventional truth (samvriti-satya) or relative truth of the manifested world.
