Parakletos

(Greek παράκλητος) "advocate, helper." 

"From above, from the world of Urania to the world of the universal parakletos [helper], vividly represented by the kalahamsa swan that flies over the waters of life, the Christic forces descended, went down the luminous tiers from  Atman ,  buddhi , superior  manas , etc... " —Samael Aun Weor,  Investigation of the Gnostic Unction (Eucharist, Communion)
