Purusha

(Sanskrit) Supreme being.

"Behind this world show, behind this physical phenomena, behind these names and
forms, behind the feelings, thoughts, emotions, sentiments, there dwells the silent
witness, thy immortal Friend and real Well-wisher, the Purusha or the World-teacher, the
invisible Power or Consciousness ." - Swami Sivananda, God Exists 

"That secondless Supreme Being, who resides in the chambers of your heart as the
Inner Ruler or Controller, who has no beginning, middle or end, is God or Atman, or
Brahman or Purusha or Chaitanya or Bhagavan or Purushottama." - Swami Sivananda
