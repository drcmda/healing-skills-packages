Dhyani Bodhisattva

Sanskrit ध्यानिबोधिसत्त्व 
Kether-Chokmah-Binah together form the Logos of the first triangle; Chesed-Geburah is what in Buddhism we call a Dhyani Bodhisattva, this is a very highly developed being. 

"When a Logos wants to come into the World, he emanates his Innermost. The Innermost then, together with the Divine Soul, is the Dhyani Bodhisattva of a Logos." - Samael Aun Weor, Manual of Practical Magic
