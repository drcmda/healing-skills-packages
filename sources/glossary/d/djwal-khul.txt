Djwal Khul

"Master D. K. (Djwal Khul) is another child of resurrection, another superhuman who has known how to take advantage of his sexual energy. This master belongs to the ray of Mercury; he helped Master H. P. Blavatsky by dictating to her a great part of The Secret Doctrine. He presently possesses the same physical body that he had in the year 1675, and death has not had power over him because he is a child of the resurrection." - Samael Aun Weor, The Seven Words 

As a resurrected master, Djwal Khul has no need of chanellers or mediums. He can speak for himself, when and where wishes. Therefore, what has been said about him in recent years is totally false.
