Dakini

(Tibetan kandroma ) A race of awakened female entities who are renowned for their great wisdom and power. Males are called Dakas (Tibetan dapo ). "Tibetan
texts dedicate a considerable amount of space in order to praise and to
disclose the beauty and grace of the Dakinis" form. They are
represented with a beautiful contexture, ruby red skin, gentle and
pensive faces, red eyes and red nails, and it is said that they exude
the tenuous fragrance of a lotus bud." - Samael Aun Weor, Occult Medicine and Practical Magic
