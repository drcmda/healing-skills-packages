Orifiel

(Hebrew יופיאל, "beauty of God") One of the seven angels. Related to Saturn. 

"What shall we now say about the old man of heaven, the lord of the law, the old Orifiel? Oh Saturn! You are the sword of justice that reaches us from heaven. In your hands is the life and estate of all humans." —Samael Aun Weor, Practical Astrology
