Osiris

An Egyptian symbol of our Innermost Being. On the Tree of Life, this is represented by the sephirah Chesed. 

" Osiris is the Father who is in secret, the particular Monad of every one." - Samael Aun Weor,  Chapter 28: The Unconscious Itself 


"You must pull the light from the darkness in the underworld so that you may reach your Father Osiris, the Innermost, your real Being..." - Samael Aun Weor,  Chapter 5: The Chapter of Bringing Along a Boat in the Underworld
