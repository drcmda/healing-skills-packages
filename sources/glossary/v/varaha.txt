Varaha

वराह , Varāha , "boar" is the avatar of the Hindu god Vishnu , in the form of a boar . Varaha is also the name of the current cosmic era. 

"...the last Kalpa was the Padma, or that of the Golden Lotus; the present one being Varaha (the "boar" incarnation, or Avatar ) ... The Varaha , however, is no simple boar, and seems to have meant at first some antediluvian lacustrine animal "delighting to sport in water." ( Vayu Purana. )" - H.P. Blavatsky, The Secret Doctrine
