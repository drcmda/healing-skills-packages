Venus

(Latin) The daughter-goddess of Jupiter; the Roman goddess of love and beauty. 

The third of the seven rays / planets. As a planetary influence, is related to "Loves, marriages, women, perfumes, music, plastic arts, dramatic arts, poetry." - Samael Aun Weor, Manual of Practical Magic. Governed by Uriel. 
Has an esoteric relation ship with Christ (the "morning star") and with Lucifer (the "Son of Dawn" from Isaiah).
