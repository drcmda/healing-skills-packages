Interdependance

"Indeed, all phenomena of nature and all objects are found intimately
and organically joined together, internally dependent upon each other
and mutually conditioning each other. Indeed, no phenomena of nature
can be integrally comprehended if we consider it isolated. Everything
is in incessant movement. Everything changes; nothing is quiet." - Samael Aun Weor, Endocrinology & Criminology 

This was taught by the Buddha Shakyamuni as pratityasamutpada, translated as Dependent Origination or the doctrine of Emptiness (the Void).
