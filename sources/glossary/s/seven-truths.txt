Seven Truths

1. Knowledge of the pains and miseries of this world, taught by Gabriel in the Polar era. 
2. Knowledge of human fragility, taught by Raphael in the Hyperborean era. 
3. Knowledge of desire and sin, taught by Uriel in the Lemurian era. 
4. Knowledge of the human mind, taught by Michael in the Atlantean era. 
5. The Tree of Knowledge, taught by Samael in the Aryan era (today). 
6. Human consciousness, to be taught by Zachariel in the Koradian era. 
7. The Tree of Life, to be taught by Gabriel to the seventh root race.
