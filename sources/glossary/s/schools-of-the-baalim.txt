Schools of the Baalim

"The schools of the Baalim were instituted in the abyss by
humanity. The schools of the Baalim consist of all the
pseudo-spiritual schools that presently exist in the world. All
such external schools are from the abyss. If man wants to leave
the abyss he must liberate his mind from all these cages. When we
penetrate into the internal worlds we find all the students of the
schools of Baalim submerged in the profound darkness of black
magic. These poor beings are searching outwardly for what they
already have inside... The Baalim are the black magicians. 
All such spiritualistic schools are filled with Fornication and
Adultery ." - Samael Aun Weor, Igneous Rose
