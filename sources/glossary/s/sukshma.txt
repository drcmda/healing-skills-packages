Sukshma

Sanskrit सूक्ष्म "small, atomic, subtle." Astral, corresponding to the fifth dimension. The astral body is sukshma sarira. The energy of that body is sukshma prana. 

"Just as you have a nervous system in the gross physical body, so also there is a nervous system in the astral body. The nervous system of the physical body is the Sthula Prana. The nervous system of the astral body is the Sukshma Prana . There is intimate connection between these two Pranas. There is interaction between these two Pranas." -Swami Sivananda
