Sardis

One of the seven churches mentioned in the Christian book of Revelation.

power of hearing the voices of those beings that live in the
supra-sensible worlds. The Church of Sardis is in the chakra of the
larynx; this chakra has sixteen petals. When the Kundalini reaches this
height, it flourishes upon our fertile lips made verb." - Samael Aun Weor, The Yellow Book
