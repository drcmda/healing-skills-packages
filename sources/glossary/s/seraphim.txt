Seraphim

( Hebrew ) A word that comes from the Hebrew root Saraph meaning “to burn.” Seraphim literally means “the burning ones.” This word is used differently by various traditions. 
1. According to the Kabbalah , Seraphim is a being related to the sephirah Geburah . 
2. According to Christianity, a Seraphim is the highest type of angel, related to the sephirah Kether .
