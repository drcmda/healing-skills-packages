Morya

"Master Morya, master of the ray of Mars, dwells in the Himalayas, at the edge of a road. He dwells in a humble house; he has innumerable disciples, and his present body has an age of more than nine hundred years. Against Master Morya, death has not and shall not succeed either, because Master Morya is a child of the resurrection of the dead, and death cannot succeed over any son of resurrection." - Samael Aun Weor, The Seven Words
