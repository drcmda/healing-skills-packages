Mind

(Sanskrit, Manas) Superior Manas ( Tiphereth ) and Inferior Manas ( Netzach ). 

"In the name of truth we must state that in synthesis we have three
minds. The first one we can and must call the Sensual Mind. The second
we shall christen with the name of Intermediate Mind. The third we
shall call the Inner Mind." - Samael Aun Weor, The Great Rebellion
