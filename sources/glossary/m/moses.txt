Moses

( Hebrew ) Actually Moshe משה; the letters Mem , "water," Shin "fire," and Hei "womb" literally mean "born of water and fire" - in other words, Moses was "born of the water and the Spirit (fire)" just as Jesus explained the Second Birth to Nicodemus. Moses is a Twice-born Master. 

Author of the Torah (the first five books of the Bible), and prophet of the Christian, Jewish, and Muslim traditions. 
A symbol of willpower, related to the sephirah Tiphereth . 
A symbol of the Superior Manas / Human Soul / Causal body , related to the sephirah Tiphereth . 
A symbol of the pineal gland. 
The Leviathan (Levi-tannyn).
