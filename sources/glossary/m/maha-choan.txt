Maha-Choan

Sanskrit for “Chief Lord.” A title or rank. “The head of a spiritual Hierarchy.” - from
the Theosophical Glossary. Can also be a reference to the Holy Spirit. 

"The seventh initiation confers upon us the degree of
“Maha-choan. The Maha-choan becomes a guardian of the Akashic
archives and director of the works of the great White Lodge." - Samael Aun Weor, The Zodiacal Course
